Code For Change

Innovate. Educate. Serve.

Code For Change is a group of high school coders who work on free projects for local organizations. Our goal is to employ the programming talent at our school to innovate and make tangable change in our community.

We work hard, so you don't have to

Our experienced developers will work around the clock to create a software solution that will meet your needs, and reduce your workload, so you can get back to doing what you do best - serving the community.

Pro Bono

For those who don't speak Latin, we'll do any project completely free. We also personally work with hosting and domain companies to ensure that these fees are as low as possible.

Helping you helps us

Code For Change is more than just a team of developers - we are organization of ambitious students. Giving your local branch a project to work on helps those students further develop their Computer Science skills.

The Process

Reach Out

Contact us over email, phone or our contact form and give some brief details about what you are looking for in the project. We'll get back to you as soon as we can and set up a time for a meeting.

Meeting

Next, we'll meet you in person to get a good feel of your needs for this project. We'll bring a rough sketch of what the project could look like based on your description.

Project Work

We'll get right to work on the project. We'll keep in touch to ask any questions we may have.

Critique

We always want to make sure the work we put out meets the needs of our clients, so we'd love to hear about any improvements you'd like us to make.

Deployment

Once you've approved our project, we'll get it in your hands immediately. Don't worry, we'll instruct you on how it works and we'll be available anytime to fix any issues that might arise.
